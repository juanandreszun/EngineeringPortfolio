Project Goal

Showcasing Various Manufacturing Projects for FSAE

Shaft Collar Mounts

I waterjetted and milled 12 shaft collar mounts to connect our nosecone to the chassis.

Pedal Welding

I welded and lathed the aluminum pedal for our IC 2022 car. I used stick welding for this project.

Jigs for Welding

I CAD'd and 3D printed these jigs that were used for welding alignment for our chassis.

Coped Tubes for Welding

I used a 4 axis lathe to cope these 4130 steel tubes for welding as the cross member for the seat in our chassis.
